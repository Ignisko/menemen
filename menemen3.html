RETURN TO HUB

A long time ago in a kitchen far, far away...

EPISODE III:
THE MENEMEN CONQUEST

It is a period of culinary unrest. IGGY and IBO, the galaxy's bravest chefs, have launched into deep space to escape the hunger that plagues the outer rim.

Their mission: to conquer the legendary Menemen sectors and harvest the ingredients for the Ultimate Feast.

But their journey is fueled by more than just hunger. They search for a MYSTERIOUS GIRL, the keeper of the Secret Sizzle, who vanished into the Spicy Nebula.

Only by harvesting the stars can they find her and fulfill their destiny...
