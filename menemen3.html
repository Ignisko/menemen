RETURN TO HUB

A long time ago in a kitchen far, far away...

EPISODE III:
THE GALACTIC FRUIT QUEST

It is a period of culinary unrest. IGGY and IBO, the galaxy's bravest chefs, have launched into deep space to escape the hunger that plagues the outer rim.

Their mission: to collect the legendary GALACTIC FRUIT and secure the ingredients for the Ultimate Star-Feast.

But their journey is fueled by more than just hunger. They search for a MYSTERIOUS GIRL, the keeper of the Sacred Sizzle, who vanished into the Spicy Nebula.

Only by harvesting the fruits of the stars can they find her and fulfill their destiny...
